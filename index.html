  • Design thinking:

      Design thinking is a process which is developed taken into consideration the problem and find the desired solution for it. The goal of design thinking is to meet people's expectations with what is technologically feasible and viable as a strategy.
      It is generally defined as an analytic and creative process that engages, create and prototype models, gather feedback and redesign.

  • Importance of design thinking:

      Design thinking is amazingly valuable in handling issues that are badly characterized or obscure, by re-outlining the issue in human-driven ways, making numerous thoughts in meetings to generate new ideas, and embracing an involved methodology in prototyping and testing.
